Burj Khalifa

The world's tallest building, a global icon

The Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, and the magnificent centerpiece of Downtown Dubai. It is a global
icon and a feat of engineering, design, and architecture. The tower features the world’s highest observation deck, the
world’s highest restaurant, and the world’s highest nightclub. It is home to the Armani Hotel Dubai and luxury
residences.




See the city from new heights




Discover the architecture and design


The Burj Khalifa was designed by the Chicago-based architectural firm, Skidmore, Owings & Merrill. The building is
inspired by the geometry of a desert flower, and it features a Y-shaped floor plan that maximizes views and natural light.
The exterior is clad in reflective glass and steel, and it features a series of setbacks that create a spiraling silhouette.
The tower is a marvel of modern engineering and a symbol of Dubai's ambition and innovation.
